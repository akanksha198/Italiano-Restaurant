Italiano offers authentic Italian recipes. These recipes all share a real Italian flavour so you can get a taste of Italy when you try them out for yourself. Seafood linguine is one of those pasta recipes, that reminds me of the many summers spent on holiday along the south coast of Puglia, Italy. Sitting in a restaurant, facing the sea, enjoying a steaming bowl of pasta brimming with the freshest seafood around. It’s absolute heaven. They offer over 50 pasta shapes, offering an eternity of pasta sauce options to satisfy all your pasta cravings.

Our chefs here at italiano belieo that end of summer tomatoes are often the best. I have several favorite appetizers which Italiano offers, baked pasta dishes, and desserts that work well being prepared hours before serving, or even the day before. A few of my favorite desserts that fit into this category are my Crostata (Jam Tart), Panna Cotta, and Tiramisu. In the past, I have had Pumpkin Tiramisu, Peach Tiramisu, Limoncello Tiramisu, and Strawberry Tiramisu. This recipe uses a combination of mixed berries, which are in season here in Michigan right now. I layer the berries with traditional Savoiardi cookies (Lady Fingers), and a creamy mascarpone custard, and is truly one of a kind. You definitely need to try this one.
