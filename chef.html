SARANSH GOILA

Saransh Goila is the first winner of Food Food Maha challenge and is the youngest TV chef known for his TV program Roti Rasta aur India aired on Food Food Channel. We met him recently at a culinary competition organised by Food and Night Life Magazine where he was anchoring the show. Here is a tete-a-tete with a rising star of the culinary world.

Birthday: February 17, 1987 | Place of Birth: Delhi | Hometown: Delhi

School/College: Manav Sthali School| First Job: The Leela, Bangalore

He is the founder of a popular Mumbai based restaurant 'Goila Butter Chicken' and author of the acclaimed food travelogue 'India on my Platter'. In 2018 he was invited to be a guest judge from India on Masterchef Australia where contestants had to cook his version of Butter Chicken. In 2013 he participated in the show, Sanjeev Kapoor Ke Kitchen Khiladi, which went on air on 16 September 2013 on Sony India. He is the host of the TV Show Roti Rasta aur India and Healthy Fridge on Food Food channel. He is based in Mumbai. In 2016 Saransh started his restaurant Goila Butter Chicken.
